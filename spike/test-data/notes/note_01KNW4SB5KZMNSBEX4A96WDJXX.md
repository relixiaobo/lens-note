传统媒体和新媒体都更倾向于报道负面新闻，因为负面事件更能立即抓住注意力。正如史蒂芬·平克所说，「好事情」往往是「今天没有坏事发生」——而这不可能成为新闻。因此，新闻天然反映的是现实中的异常，而非常态，不能代表真实的现实世界。(Both traditional and new media gravitate toward negative news because it captures attention faster. As Steven Pinker notes, good things are simply the absence of bad ones — and that cannot be news. So news inherently reflects abnormality, not normality, and cannot represent reality as a whole.)

The 'ambient UX ignores how users switch modes when an agent encounters ambiguity' gap (noted in note_01KNW55PVTPAR5BZEGN4R3J2V0) is partially addressed by the mechanism of proactive help-seeking: the agent itself initiates the switch back to synchronous interaction when it needs human input. This means Ambient UX is not purely asynchronous — it has an escape valve into synchronous mode, triggered by the agent, not the user. The trust architecture of background agents may depend on this escape valve being reliable and not overused.

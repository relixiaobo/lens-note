Almost all AI browsers have no viable business model. They traded being Chromium-based for being Chromium-based AND financially dependent on AI providers for API calls. They are not building the browser, and they are not building the AI — they are a layer on top of both. The deeper risk: nothing is stopping Google from integrating the same AI capabilities directly into Chrome.

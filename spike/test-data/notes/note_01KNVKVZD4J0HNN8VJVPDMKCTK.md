The meta-methods insight is the deepest philosophical point in the Bitter Lesson: don't build in what you've discovered — build in the capacity to discover. Human knowledge is a finished artifact; a discovery process is generative. Any fixed knowledge caps the system at what humans currently understand. A learning process is unbounded. This reframes the entire AI alignment between 'knowledge-in' and 'learning-from' as a question of whether you want a ceiling or a horizon.

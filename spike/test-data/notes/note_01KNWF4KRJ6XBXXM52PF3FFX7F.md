A new taxonomy classifies user messages into Asking (seeking information), Doing (producing output/task), and Expressing (views/feelings). Distribution: ~49% Asking, ~40% Doing, ~11% Expressing. As of July 2025, ~56% of work-related messages are Doing, and nearly three-quarters of those are Writing tasks — notable because writing is universal to white-collar work and generative AI is distinctively capable of long-form output.

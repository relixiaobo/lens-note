The AI startup prisoner's dilemma: rational actors must pursue vertical AI (human-knowledge-enhanced, short-term viable) even while knowing that general AI (computation-scaling) will eventually obsolete them. This is a structurally trapped position — not irrational, because the agent needs to survive now, but collectively suboptimal. The vertical AI window is real, but its moat dissolves the moment base models improve. The dilemma is unsolvable within the startup's planning horizon.

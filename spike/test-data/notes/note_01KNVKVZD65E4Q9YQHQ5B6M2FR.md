Computation is the only confirmed monotonically growing resource in AI. Human knowledge is effectively fixed on a decade-scale (it grows, but slowly relative to compute). This asymmetry means any approach bottlenecked by human knowledge will eventually be overtaken by approaches that can exploit growing compute. The strategic implication: bet on the growing resource, not the fixed one — regardless of which produces better results today.

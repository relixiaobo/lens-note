The 'collect usage data first, decide pricing later' framing of indirect monetization as a temporary strategy is a concrete instantiation of the open question about where LLMs' true commercial value lies. The implication: for most products, the answer to that question is not known in advance — the data-collection phase IS the mechanism for discovering it. Indirect monetization is not just a revenue strategy; it is an epistemic strategy for mapping AI value onto business metrics before committing to a pricing architecture.

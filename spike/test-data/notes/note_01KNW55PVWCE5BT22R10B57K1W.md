The four agent UX paradigms (Spreadsheet, Collaborative, Ambient, Generative) can be mapped along two axes: (1) degree of human attention required during execution (high → low), and (2) locus of control (user-driven → agent-driven). Spreadsheet UX: high attention, user-driven. Collaborative UX: high attention, shared control. Ambient UX: low attention, agent-driven. Generative UI: variable attention, agent-driven (the UI itself is the agent's output). This framing reveals that the choice of UX paradigm is actually a choice about the human-agent power dynamic — who is doing the work, and who is supervising.

镜像世界（Mirror World）的商业模式：广告模式不会消失，但注意力可能不再是唯一变现方式。在镜像世界中，人们的注意力会被密切追踪甚至操纵，用户极易被榨取。长远来看，可能走向类似宽带费的按月订阅模式。(The Mirror World business model: advertising won't disappear, but attention may no longer be the sole revenue mechanism. Attention will be closely tracked and potentially manipulated, making users highly exploitable. Long-term, a monthly subscription model like broadband fees seems likely.)

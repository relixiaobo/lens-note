AI 对白领工作的替代是以「无感接入」的形式悄然融入现有办公流程，而非颠覆整个工作方式。AI 成为了脑力劳动领域可以 7x24 不眠不休的核心齿轮，取代了以往作为这个「零件」的白领本身——白领作为人是需要休息的，这一约束曾迫使企业优化脑力劳动时必须将人的局限纳入考量。(AI's displacement of white-collar work doesn't require redefining work — it quietly inserts into existing office workflows. AI becomes the 24/7 tireless cog in the knowledge-work machine, replacing the white-collar worker who, as a human, needed rest — a constraint that once forced organizations to design around human limits.)

即使面对全新的事物，我们也会借助一生的经验来加以理解。我们所看到的几乎一切都深深融入了过往的经验——轮廓、光影、纹理和图案——以至我们很难想象能真正孤立地看到任何东西。(Even when confronted with entirely new things, we make sense of them through a lifetime of accumulated experience. Almost everything we see is deeply fused with prior experience — contours, light, texture, pattern — such that we can barely conceive of seeing anything in true isolation.)

在 AI 应用中，最常见的错误之一是「先构建复杂流程，最后才想到做评估」。很多企业投入大量资源搭建复杂 AI 系统，却忽略评估，直到上线后才发现效果不佳，浪费大量时间和精力。(One of the most common mistakes in AI deployment: building complex pipelines first and only thinking about evaluation last. Companies pour resources into sophisticated AI systems, neglect evaluation, then discover poor performance post-launch.)
